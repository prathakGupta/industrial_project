Ransomeware attacks

Ransomware attacks typically begin with a phishing email or other social engineering tactics that trick employees into clicking on a link or downloading a malicious attachment. Once the ransomware is installed on the system, it begins to encrypt files and data on the network, making them inaccessible to the organization. The attacker then demands a ransom payment in exchange for the decryption key.
